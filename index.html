About Me

I am a front-end web developer, leveraging five years of experience as a practicing general dentist to best serve clients and users in developing interactive and engaging programs. Having completed a certification program in full stack development at Rice University, I developed the skills to use JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap, React and other tools to develop responsive, mobile-friendly applications.

I am passionate about precision in my work, solving complex problems, and working with others to build concise, highly functional code. I am excited to continue to expand my skill sets, obtain additional certifications, and grow as a team-player and developer. In my free time I like to play golf, watch sports, play video games, and traverse the internet in search of ways to better myself as a programmer.
